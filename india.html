Seven Wonders of the World

Taj Mahal

Originally built as a mausoleum for the beloved wife of a Mughal emperor, the Taj Mahal is possibly the greatest example of Muslim art existing anywhere in the world, and also of the unique Mughal architectural style that combines classic Persian and Indian features. This impressive complex of structures, crowned by its universally recognized white marble dome, represents a feat of architecture, design, and art that cannot be found anywhere else in the world.

  • Height: 73 meters
  • Location: Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Pradesh
  • it is the pride of India and more importantly, an epitome of love. Built as early as in the 16th century.
